CEVE 543: Environmental Data Science

Iif you can program a computer, you have direct access to the deepest, most fundamental ideas in statistics (John Rauser). In this course, an engaging, efficient, and accessible learning environment and innovative interactive computing will help you build your understanding of environmental data science, preparing you to be an effective producer and skeptical consumer of environmental data analyses.
